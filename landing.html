What it does

Your engine.
Your coach.

AfriChess combines a battle-tested 2050 ELO engine with a natural language coaching layer. Not just the best move — but why it's best.

♟
Play Mode

Go head-to-head against the engine. Drag and drop pieces, play as White or Black, flip the board. A genuine challenge for club players.

⊞
Analysis Board

Paste any FEN and see the engine's best move highlighted instantly. Use it to study openings, middlegames, or endgame studies.

◈
AI Coach

Every position comes with a full breakdown: assessment, best move explained in plain English, strategic plan, and a key chess principle.

≈
Eval Bar

Real-time centipawn evaluation. Know exactly who's winning and by how much — the same data grandmasters use to study.

⟳
Game Analysis

Submit your full PGN and get a coaching report identifying the turning point, the critical mistake, and how to fix it.

◻
API Access

Integrate the engine into your own apps. REST API with simple key auth. Ideal for developers building chess tools or bots.
